MorphyHair

Problem

Textured hair support is scattered.

There isn’t one platform that brings together textured hair information, tutorials, inspiration, and shopping.

What people do today

  • Tutorials → TikTok / YouTube
  • Inspiration → Instagram / Pinterest
  • Information → blogs / forums
  • Products → separate shops

Result

People waste time searching and still don’t know what works for their hair.

Why this matters

  • Hard to compare options quickly
  • Hard to learn consistently
  • Hard to buy the right products with confidence

Fix

One platform for textured hair.

MorphyHair lets people browse a large variety of textured hair info, tutorials, inspiration, and soft products — together.

Core flow

  • Learn — info + tutorials organized clearly
  • Get inspired — browse styles, save favorites
  • Buy — soft products connected to the style/tutorial
