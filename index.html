The problem

  • Textured hair inspo is everywhere — but it’s hard to know what works for your hair type.
  • Style photos don’t tell you the real requirements (hair needed, time, maintenance, skill level).
  • Most discovery tools aren’t built around textured hair realities (protective styles, installs, braids, wigs).

What we’re building

A style discovery + saving experience designed for textured hair: explore grids of looks, filter by vibe, and learn what each style actually needs.

(Salon discovery and marketplace features can come later — right now we’re validating discovery + saving.)
